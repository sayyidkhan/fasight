How it Works?

1. Answer simple questions

Make a will on the website by answering simple questions, we also have a image recognition software, which will make the setup of the will even more simpler.

2. Will is sent out and activated at later date.

After the digitization of the will, the platform will sent out the link to the guarantors, to be accessed that the later date when the account holder have deceased.

3. Make the will digital-binding

Through the use of e-signatures(bio-metric thumbprint), we can digitize the experience of the will for the whole legacy.

4. Access the funds

With a legal binding digital will, one can visit the respective financial institutions to get the fund re-structured according the to the next generation needs.
